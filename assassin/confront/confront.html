The Shadow's Vengeance

With the conspiracy unraveling, a new challenge emerges. From the shadows step three formidable foes, each embodying a unique terror and strength.

Apex Predators

The Warlock, wielding chaos magic, is a threat like no other. His arcane attacks are deadly, but his awareness is focused on the mystical, leaving him vulnerable to physical strikes.

The Dark Knight, a brute force in heavy armor, would be a formidable opponent in a direct confrontation, but your agility and stealth provide you with an advantage.

The Beast of Shadows, a creature of nightmares, is elusive and terrifying. Its formless nature makes it resistant to straightforward attacks, requiring cunning and precision to defeat.

  • Ambush the Warlock
  • Outmaneuver the Dark Knight
  • Hunt the Beast of Shadows

As an Assassin, your path is one of shadows and silence. The decisions you make now will echo through the annals of time.
