Agility Against Strength

The Dark Knight stands before you, a behemoth clad in shadowed armor. He wields a sword that could cleave stone. Your agility and cunning are your greatest weapons against his brute strength.

You dart in and out of his reach, your blades leaving trails of silver in the air. Each time he swings his massive sword, you're a ghost, a wisp of smoke that he cannot catch.

As the Knight grows weary, you find an opening. With a flurry of swift strikes, you target the chinks in his armor. He stumbles, falls, and the ground shakes upon his defeat.
