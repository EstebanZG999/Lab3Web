Shadow Against Magic

In a dim chamber, the Warlock awaits, his eyes gleaming with malevolent power. He begins a chant, summoning eldritch energies, but you use the shadows to your advantage, approaching unseen.

As he unleashes a torrent of dark magic, you deftly move through the shadows, each step calculated to avoid detection. You wait for the moment when his focus peaks and his defenses are momentarily lowered.

In that split second, you strike. Leaping from the darkness, daggers drawn, you close the gap. The Warlock turns, shock etched on his face, just as your blade finds its mark. With a whisper, he collapses, the threat of his dark magic extinguished.
