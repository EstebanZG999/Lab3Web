Confronting the Beast of Shadows

The Beast of Shadows is an enigma, its form constantly shifting and changing. You realize that direct attacks might be futile. Instead, you use your cunning to outmaneuver the creature.

Utilizing smoke bombs and traps, you create diversions, confusing the Beast. You strike from the shadows, your blades slicing through its dark form. Each hit seems to weaken it, its shadowy essence starting to wane.

In a final act of cunning, you lure the Beast into a trap imbued with ancient runes, sealing its movements. With the Beast immobilized, you deliver the final blow, banishing it back into the darkness from which it came.
