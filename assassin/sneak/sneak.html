Sneak Through the Shadows

Cloaked in darkness, you glide through the underbelly of Eldoria. The shadows are your allies, the silence your accomplice. But tonight, the shadows whisper of danger, and the silence is broken by the steps of pursuing guards.

In the Assassin's Guild, one learns that shadows are not just absence of light, but a canvas for those who operate from the unseen.

Examine your Assassin's tools

Daggers, poison vials, grappling hook, and the trusty smoke bombs - tools of your deadly trade.

A guard rounds the corner ahead; you press against the wall, your breath a silent mist. As he passes, you slip behind him, a ghost in human form. But your freedom is short-lived; a squadron blocks your path to safety.

Your choices here are a matter of life or death. Fade into the night, or fight and risk it all? The story continues...
