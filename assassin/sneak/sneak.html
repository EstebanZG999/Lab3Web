The Silent Blade

After escaping the tight grip of your pursuers, you find yourself faced with a greater challenge. Three ominous figures emerge from the darkness, blocking your path to freedom.

Deadly Encounters

The Warlock's magic is potent, but his focus on sorcery makes him blind to your stealthy approach. A silent takedown may be possible.

The Dark Knight, a towering behemoth, relies on brute strength. Your speed and agility could turn this encounter to your advantage.

The Beast of Shadows, a creature of pure darkness, eludes even the keenest of eyes. Its formless nature makes it a unique and dangerous adversary.

  • Assassinate the Warlock
  • Evade the Dark Knight
  • Vanquish the Beast of Shadows

Your journey through the shadows is fraught with peril, but your skills as an Assassin have prepared you for this moment. The fate of Aetheria is in your hands.
