The Silent Takedown of the Warlock

In a chamber echoing with dark chants, you find the Warlock. His focus is on a complex incantation, unaware of your silent approach. Shadows cloak your movement as you edge closer, daggers at the ready.

The Warlock unleashes a volley of shadow bolts, but you anticipated this. Using a series of swift rolls and dodges, you evade his attacks, each move bringing you closer. You notice a momentary lapse in his concentration - your cue.

With lethal precision, you strike from behind. Before he can react, your dagger finds its mark. The Warlock's spell falters, his grip on the dark energies waning, and he collapses, defeated by your shadowy prowess.
