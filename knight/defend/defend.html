Defender of Eldoria

As you stand victorious upon the battered walls of Eldoria, a new threat approaches. Three dark champions, each more menacing than the last, make their way towards the city.

Battle of Legends

The Warlock, though a formidable foe for a Sorcerer, could be vulnerable to a direct assault. His reliance on magic is his weakness.

The Dark Knight is your equal in combat, a mirror of your own martial prowess. This battle will be one for the ages.

The Beast of Shadows is an entity of pure malice, a foe that even the most skilled Assassin would fear. Its ethereal nature makes it a challenging opponent.

  • Challenge the Warlock
  • Confront the Dark Knight
  • Face the Beast of Shadows

Your sword has defended Eldoria against countless foes. Now, you must face your greatest challenge yet. The destiny of a Knight is shaped by such battles.
