The Arcane Guardian

The magical wards you erected shimmer with power, but a new threat looms. Three dark champions step through the veil of shadows, each one a grave threat to the world's balance.

Ultimate Challenge

The Warlock, a being of pure magic, challenges your understanding of the arcane. His power seems vast, but you know the secrets to unraveling spells.

The Dark Knight wields a sword that thirsts for blood. His combat prowess is great, but his resistance to magic is lacking.

The Beast of Shadows, a creature beyond physical form, resists conventional attacks. It's a foe that would normally require a more direct approach.

  • Confront the Warlock
  • Overwhelm the Dark Knight
  • Banish the Beast of Shadows

Your wisdom and power are being tested as never before. The path you choose will define the future of magic itself.
